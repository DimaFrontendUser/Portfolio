Introduction

The Kola Reserve is a new and exciting operation on the Easternmost point of Kola Peninsula. The Kola Reserve fishery comprises the Lumbovka and Kachkovka Rivers (later Olenka will be developed to the program).The aim of the Reserve is to provide wonderful catch and release fly-fishing for wild Atlantic salmon, and to work in partnership with the limited number of fly anglers that will be invited to visit the Reserve every season to sustain and conserve the fishery.

All the lucky anglers that fish the Kola Reserve will be required to leave only footprints, and take only memories. Using only barbless single and double hooks and practicing a strict 100% catch and release policy, the anglers will be invited to fish the Reserve throughout the summer and autumn months, providing data that will provide valuable in assessing the ongoing trend of run density on the rivers.
